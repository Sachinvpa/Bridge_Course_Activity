Step 2 · One By One

Picking up Numbers

  1. Number papers are kept on the table in front.
  2. One boy and one girl come forward at the same time — in order, from the front of each row.
  3. Pick whichever number paper you like from the table.
  4. Walk back to your own row and stand at the back of the line.
  5. The next one boy and one girl step up. Keep going till everyone holds a number.

Step 3 · Regroup

Row Formation

Everyone who picked number 1 makes one row. Number 2s make the next. Same for every number — until the whole room stands in 10 rows.

Step 4 · You're a Team Now

Team Formation

Look at your row — each row is now one team. The people standing with you are your teammates for the whole Bridge Course.

Step 5 · Identity

Team Name

  1. Huddle up and give your team a name — short, loud, and easy to chant.
  2. Choose one team leader — the person who will speak and collect for the team.
  3. When the timer ends, every leader announces the team name to the room.
You have 5 minutes. Name first, leader next — go!

How It Works

Game Information

  1. Each team gets one magazine paper. Cut it into 15 different shapes.
  2. After cutting, the team leader comes to the table and drops the shapes into the different boxes.
  3. Put at least 1 shape in each box — that covers 10 boxes. The remaining 5 shapes go into any boxes you like.
  4. There will be activities every day — your team's rank is displayed and changes daily, based on performance.

Final Round · The Hunt

Reform your original magazine sheet

  1. Go to each team — search and request for your shapes.
  2. Collect every shape and put your original magazine sheet back together.
  3. The team that completes first is Rank 1 — and the list goes till Rank 10.
Ask politely, trade smartly — every team is holding someone's pieces.
